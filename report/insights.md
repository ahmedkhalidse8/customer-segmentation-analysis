# Customer Segmentation Insights

## Overview
This analysis segmented customers based on annual income and spending score using K-Means clustering.

## Key Findings

### Low Income High Spenders
These customers show strong purchasing behavior despite lower income levels.
They may respond well to promotions, discounts, and loyalty programs.

### Balanced Customers
These customers represent a stable customer base with moderate spending.
Retention strategies and personalized recommendations could increase engagement.

### Moderate Income Moderate Spenders
These customers demonstrate cautious spending behavior.
Targeted marketing campaigns and product suggestions could increase their purchasing activity.

### High Income Low Spenders
This segment has high purchasing power but low spending behavior.
Premium marketing campaigns and exclusive offers could activate this group.

## Business Opportunities

- Loyalty programs for high-spending segments
- Targeted promotions for moderate spenders
- Premium campaigns for high-income customers
